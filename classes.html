A Step-by-Step Guide to Enrollment

New Student

Inquire & Pay Assessment Fee

Schedule the assessment and settle the fee at the cashier. Assessment may be done the same day or as scheduled.

Take the Assessment Exam

Results are released the same day with tuition details and document requirements.

Proceed to Registrar’s Office

Get assessed for fees and fill up the admission form.

Pay Required Fees

Settle necessary payments at the BBIA Extension Office (Cashier).

Receive Certificate of Enrollment

Present the official receipt at the Registrar’s Office to obtain your certificate.

Old Student

Go to Registrar’s Office

Proceed for assessment of fees and fill up the admission form.

Get the Payment Slip

Obtain a payment slip from the Accounting Office; pay now or schedule later.

Pay Required Fees

Settle payment at the BBIA Extension Office (Cashier).

Submit Receipt

Submit the official receipt at the Registrar’s Office and receive updated tuition info.

Receive Certificate of Enrollment

Get your certificate and book once payment is confirmed.
