Story Behind the BBIA Success

The first piece in place.

Founded in 2016 by the Board of Directors, Building Blocks International Academy began with a simple mission: to provide superior instruction that helps children realize their full potential and become contributing members of society.

We launched a Summer Camp in 2016 and soon introduced a Preschool program under the guidance of the Department of Education. Since then we've expanded grade levels, programs, and facilities while keeping student-centered learning at the core of everything we do.

Stacking Stronger Foundations.

In early 2017 and 2018 respectively, BBIA received its accreditation from the International Preschool Curriculum and Bureau of Immigration, which resulted to a more progressive and responsive perspective embodied by the academy.

Academic year 2017-2018 marked the many firsts of the academic terms of having various school activities. It started with 19 pioneer students, with 6 faculty and staff, headed by the School Administrator.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a world class learning school that motivated curiosity and independence. To help our children realize their full potential and become contributing members to our nation and to the world.

Our Mission

To provide positive learning environment in partnership with parents, global institutions, and organization. To provide innovative education training program towards the development of dynamic individuals with strong moral values and passion for life long learning.
